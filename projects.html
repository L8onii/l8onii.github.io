League of Legends Capstone Project

  • This program is designed to help players identify champions by lane roles—Top, Mid, Jungle, ADC (Bottom), and Support—and make informed choices based on several factors: champion difficulty, cost (in Blue Essence or RP), preferred lane, and performance metrics like KDA (Kill/Death/Assist ratio). While it won’t guarantee victory, the analysis and visualizations provided will offer deeper insights into champion stats and help you choose champions that fit your playstyle.
